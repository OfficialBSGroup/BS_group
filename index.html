Founder & CEO

BS GROUP

Meet Bhavesh Singla, an accomplished entrepreneur and CEO of BS Group, a leading brand management and digital marketing company. Bhavesh graduated from Symbiosis College of Arts and Commerce with a BCom in Marketing specialisation and went on to complete his MBA in Marketing specialisation from Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies.
With over 8 years of experience in the industry, Bhavesh has worked with leading advertising agencies and honed his skills in branding, advertising, and digital marketing. He is passionate about helping businesses grow and has a deep understanding of the latest marketing trends and technologies.
Bhavesh's commitment to excellence and innovative thinking has been recognized by the industry, and he was recently awarded CEO of the Year 2021 by Business Connect. He was also featured in the Business Connect Magazine's September 2021 edition.
In addition to leading BS Group, Bhavesh founded Naya Gurukul in 2021. Naya Gurukul is an online learning platform that offers courses on branding, digital marketing, and entrepreneurship. Bhavesh is passionate about sharing his knowledge and experience with others and is always willing to help aspiring entrepreneurs.
As a visionary leader, Bhavesh is dedicated to empowering businesses with the tools and resources they need to succeed in today's fast-paced and ever-evolving market. His expertise and experience make him a sought-after speaker at industry events, and he is recognized as a thought leader in the field of branding and digital marketing.
If you're looking to take your business to the next level and build a brand that truly stands out, Bhavesh Singla and the team at BS Group are here to help. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you achieve your marketing goals.
